About me

I'm Ryan Huang,
Software Engineer & Photographer

I graduated from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo in 2016 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I also consider myself an enthusiast in both basketball and photography. I've been programming since 13 and am currently working full time at a tech company in Silicon Valley. I started my non-pro basketball career in elementary and enjoys every aspect of NBA. I've always had interest in photography ever since I took my first photography class in high school. Now that I have time to spare, it has become my number one hobby.
